<p><strong>The CMP Polishing Material Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>CMP Slurry</li><li>CMP Pad</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The CMP polishing material market is primarily categorized into two types: CMP slurry and CMP pads. CMP slurry consists of a mixture of chemical agents and abrasives, used to remove material and achieve a smooth surface on semiconductor wafers and other substrates during the chemical mechanical polishing process. CMP pads, on the other hand, are the physical substrates that support the polishing process, providing a surface for the slurry to interact with the material being polished, ensuring uniformity and effectiveness.</p></p>
