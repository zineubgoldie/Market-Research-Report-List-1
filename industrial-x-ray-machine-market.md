<p><ul><li>Analog X-Ray Machine</li><li>Digital X-Ray Machine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Industrial X-Ray Machine market is primarily segmented into Analog and Digital X-Ray Machines. Analog X-Ray Machines utilize traditional film-based technology, producing images via X-ray exposure on photographic film. They are often more affordable but have limitations in image quality and processing time. In contrast, Digital X-Ray Machines employ advanced sensor technology to create immediate digital images, offering clearer resolution and improved image processing capabilities. Digital machines enhance efficiency and accuracy in industrial inspections, making them increasingly popular in various sectors.</p></p>
