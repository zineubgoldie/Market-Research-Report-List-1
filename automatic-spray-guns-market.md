<p><p>The automatic spray guns market comprises two main types: high-pressure and low-pressure paint spray guns. High-pressure paint spray guns operate at elevated pressures, ensuring fine atomization of paint for smooth finishes, making them ideal for large-scale industrial applications. Conversely, low-pressure paint spray guns utilize reduced pressure to minimize overspray and improve transfer efficiency, making them suitable for detailed work and smaller projects. Both types cater to varying needs in sectors like automotive, aerospace, and furniture manufacturing.</p></p>
