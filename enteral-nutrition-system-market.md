<p><p>The enteral nutrition system market comprises two primary types: enteral feeding pumps and enteral feeding tubes. Enteral feeding pumps are devices that deliver liquid nutrition directly into the gastrointestinal tract at controlled rates, facilitating precise nutritional management for patients unable to consume food orally. Enteral feeding tubes, on the other hand, are flexible tubes inserted into the stomach or intestines to provide nourishment directly to patients with swallowing difficulties or specific medical conditions. Both segments are essential for improving patient outcomes in various clinical settings.</p></p>
