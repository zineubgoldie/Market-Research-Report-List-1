<p><p>The Monocrystalline Silicon Solar Cell market is primarily categorized into two types: Planar Monocrystalline Silicon Solar Cells and Grooved Buried Gate Electrode Monocrystalline Silicon Cells. Planar cells feature a flat design that maximizes light absorption and efficiency, making them widely used in residential and commercial applications. In contrast, Grooved Buried Gate Electrode cells incorporate a unique grooved structure that enhances charge collection and reduces resistive losses, offering improved performance in higher-efficiency solar technologies. Both types contribute to the growing demand for renewable energy solutions.</p></p>
